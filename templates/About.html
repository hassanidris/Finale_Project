About this site

The Fly Travel Guide (FTG) is the flagship digital consumer brand within the Columbus Travel Media portfolio. A comprehensive guide to the worlds best travel destinations, its print heritage stretches back 30 years, with the online portal reaching its 19-year anniversary in 2018. Available in English and Spanish versions. It covers all aspects, from cities to airports, cruise ports to ski and beach resorts, attractions to events, and it also includes weekly travel news, features and quizzes. Updated every day by a dedicated global editorial team.
